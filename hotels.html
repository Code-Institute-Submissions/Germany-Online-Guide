Hotels in Germany

Steigenberger Frankfurter Hof

Enjoy the fine dining and 24 hours room service! If you would like to do some sports to stay healthy, you could join their fitness area or jog at the beautiful Main River. In case you require some time to relax, go to their comfortable spa. You could even get a conference room for a meeting with your business people and eat delicious food at their restaurant. For more information, please click here.

Hotel Ambassador Hamburg-Hammerbrook

Stay healthy by joining their fitness area and spa and enjoy the comfortable atmosphere with personal service! Eat at their own restaurant and get a children's play room. In the rooms, a TV and hair dryer are offered. For more information, please click here.

Hotel Alexander Plaza - Berlin

The hotel is located at a peaceful place in the middle of Berlin, nearby the Museum Island and historical buidlings. Go to their new wellness area and spacious sauna to relax and get rid of stress and stay healthy and fit by joing their gym! Moreover, eat a delicious breakfast rich of flavours. For more information, please click here.

Goldener Karpfen - Romantik Hotel & Restaurant - Fulda

Enjoy different types of meals everyday made with fresh ingredients and relax in their comfort room with traditional and antique stocked styles. Furthermore, big conference rooms with 10 to 120 chairs and the pavillion with an inviting garden are offered. For more information, please click here.
