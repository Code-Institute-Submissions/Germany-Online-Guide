About Germany.

Germany has a population of higher than 80 million, therefore being the second most populated country in Europe.

It is said that 91.5% Germans live in Germany, while the second highest ethnic group is Turkish at 2.4%. German is mostly spoken in West Germany and is an official language in Switzerland and Austria. It is probably the sixth most spoken language in the world and is spoken by around 90 million people.

Moreover, there are different dialects, for instance High German (Hochdeutsch) and Middle German. High German was spoken before Middle German was spoken in 1100AD. Middle German is the language that the Bible was translated in by Martin Luther.

The modern high German dialects that descends from the old Middle German dialects, is mostly spoken in the central and southern areas of Germany, Austria and Switzerland. Other dialects are Low German, which is still spoken in some nothern areas, Alemannic dialect, which comes from High German and Yiddish, which was spoken by German Jews since the middle ages and also comes from High German.

Learn About German Culture and History!

There were many popular german composer, such as Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, Ludwig van Beethoven and Richard Wagner. Furthermore, there is lots of interesting architecture from a wide variety of time periods and famous houses, in which poets, such as the Goethe used to live in.

Why Travel To Germany?

Discover historical, big, breathtaking places in Germany and learn about the german culture and traditions!

Explore the famous castles with beautiful stories behind them, learn about the development of their cities and cars, and visit historcal places, like the Berlin Wall, and enjoy famous events, such the christmasmarkets!

Furthermore, enjoy the wide range of international entertainment in Frankfurt on the streets, theatres and restaurants throughout the big city. Another example of an interesting city is Hamburg, where one could have fun doing things like sportif beach games or visiting the 'Kunsthalle' (Art hall).
