The German National Anthem

The national anthem starts at 11 seconds.

The german national anthem was composed by Austrian Joseph Haydn in the year 1796 for Kaiser Francis II. However, it was used as Germany's national anthem, centuries later, in 1922. By that time, the lyrics was changed by a nationalist, poet and university professor August Heinrich Hoffmann von Fallersleben.
